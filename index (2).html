Pipeline

How the prediction gets made

input

Clean & encode

Fix missing billing values, encode categorical fields, scale numeric ones.

balance

Correct for imbalance

SMOTE oversampling so the rarer churn class isn't ignored by the model.

train

Compare 4 models

Logistic Regression, Decision Tree, Random Forest, Gradient Boosting.

select

Score by ROC–AUC

Not accuracy alone — precision, recall, and F1 matter more on imbalanced data.

serve

Predict live

Best model is saved and served through the Streamlit app for real-time scoring.

Findings

What actually drives churn here

Month-to-month contracts churn far more than annual ones
New customers (low tenure) are the highest-risk group
Fiber customers without tech support churn more
Electronic check payers churn more than auto-pay users
